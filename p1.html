"Help me Obi-wan Kenobi, You're my only Hope."

The goal of this project was to create a message you want to send out to space, in hopes that another intelligent life will find and interpret it. Putting a sci-fi twist on the project, I decided to use Aurebesh in my graphic because it is a widely used language in the Star Wars universe, so perhaps aliens will recognize it- we can hope! I embedded graphics that hint towards our planet dying, as QR codes in which our alien "penpals" will have to decode. The largest QR code contains a quote by Princess Leia Organa, sending an SOS. If the Aurebesh language can't be translated by these aliens, lets hope that they can understand the pictograms.
