Project 2

Exquisite Corpse

This was a cooperative project in which I and a partner collaborated to complete an exquisite corpse. We took inspiration from historical Japanese art, Steampunk, DaVinci, and works of HP Lovecraft. Depicated is our take of a steampunk Samurai (who is a mixture of man, machine, and creature) and a Ninja (part tiger) facing off against our twist on Cthulu (wearing a top hat because he is fashionable like that).
